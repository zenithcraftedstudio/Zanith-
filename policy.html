❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use these patterns on Etsy or POD platforms?
Yes! Commercial license is included with every purchase. You can sell unlimited products on Etsy, Redbubble, Society6, Amazon, or any other platform.
How do I get my digital files after payment?
After payment is confirmed (within 24h), we email the download link directly to the email address you provided at checkout. Check your spam folder if you don't see it!
Can I request a custom color variation?
Yes! Email us with the pattern name and your desired color palette. Custom color variations start at $15 and are delivered within 2–3 business days.
What if I need the file in SVG or AI format?
Contact us after purchase — we can provide SVG, AI, or EPS format for most geometric patterns at no extra charge.
Do you offer bulk discounts?
Yes! Buy 5+ patterns and get 20% off. Buy 10+ and get 30% off. Contact us before placing your order to arrange the discount code.
My pattern looks pixelated when I print — help!
Make sure you're using the full-size PNG file (3600×3600px, 300dpi) and not the preview JPG. Also ensure your software is set to the correct resolution before exporting.
Can I use the patterns for fabric printing at a local print shop?
Absolutely! Commercial license covers physical fabric printing. You can bring our files to any print shop or use online fabric printing services like Spoonflower.
How do I contact you for support?
Email us at hello@zanithcraftedstudio.com or message us on Instagram/Zalo. We respond within 24 hours on business days.
