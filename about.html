Our Story

Patterns Made with Heart & Purpose

We are Zanith Crafted Studio — a small independent design studio creating handcrafted seamless patterns and print-on-demand goods for makers, sellers, and dreamers worldwide.

🌿
Who We Are

Born from a Love of Craft & Color

Zanith Crafted Studio was founded with a simple belief: beautiful patterns should be accessible to everyone — from independent Etsy sellers to emerging POD brands and textile designers.

Every design in our collection is hand-crafted with attention to detail, colour harmony, and commercial versatility. We draw inspiration from folk art traditions, botanical worlds, cottagecore aesthetics, and modern graphic design — weaving them together into patterns that are timeless yet fresh.

Our studio is built on passion for surface design and a genuine desire to help creative entrepreneurs build beautiful, cohesive brands without the cost of custom design commissions.
